Welcome to KILOJI

Your transport management software, 2.0 edition

KILOJI is the integrated transport management solution by SAWORI Holding & Partners SARL. It centralizes contracts, the fleet, drivers, routes, maintenance (CMMS), HSEQ compliance, operations and invoicing, with an Electronic Document Management (EDM/DMS) system that links all document-producing modules. This 2.0 edition consolidates the original guide and integrates the improved modules as well as the new modules.

Login & Deployment

Access your KILOJI workspace securely

🌐
Login URL: kiloji.com — accessible from any web browser (Chrome recommended).
🔗 kiloji.com

Tools required before first login

📧

Active Gmail account

Required to verify the email address.

🔑

Google Authenticator

Two-factor authentication (2FA) on a second phone.

🌐

Web browser

Chrome or a modern browser, connection ≥ 1 Mbit/s.

🏢

SAWORI credentials

Email and temporary password provided by your administrator.

🆕 First login (new account)

1
Open the login window

Go to kiloji.com, enter your email or phone and your temporary password, then click Validate.

2
Verify your email address Required

In Gmail, open the KILOJI email and click “Verify email address”. The temporary password is shown there.

3
Scan the 2FA QR code

Refresh the KILOJI page: a QR code appears. On a second phone, open Google Authenticator → + → “Scan a code”.

4
Enter the 6-digit code

Copy the code shown in Authenticator into the “Verification code” field, then click “Activate verification”. Your workspace opens.

⚠️
Enter your credentials correctly to avoid account lockout. The 2FA code is strictly confidential — never share it.

🔄 Regular login (existing account)

Open kiloji.com
→
Copy the 2FA code (Authenticator)
→
Paste & Validate
→
User workspace
⏱️
Features depend on your rights and permissions. A new 2FA authentication is required 1 hour after logout.

EDM — Electronic Document Management

Centralize, trace and find all your documents

📂
The EDM links all of KILOJI’s document-producing modules (contracts, templates, letters, procedures, sanction documents, invoices, exports) within a single document workspace — organized, searchable and traceable.

Electronic Document Management turns scattered files into a structured library. Each document has a document record (reference, type, status, version, date, domain, location), can be searched, filtered, versioned, exported (PDF/Excel) and archived then restored, respecting rights and confidentiality.

EDM capabilities

🗂️

Tree library

Folders by domain and module, breadcrumb navigation.

🏷️

Metadata & references

Unique reference, type, status, version, date, domain, location.

🕓

Version control

Revision history (v1.0 → v2.0) and update tracking.

🔎

Search & filters

Full-text search and filters by type, status and domain.

🗃️

Archives & restore

Archive obsolete documents and restore them in one click.

⬇️

Import / Export PDF·Excel

Upload local files and export documents and lists.

🔐

Rights & confidentiality

Access based on each user’s and carrier’s permissions.

⭐

Favorites & recent

Quickly find documents you viewed or starred.

Using the EDM — step by step

1
Upload or draft a document

Import a file from local storage or draft it in the built-in editor (contract, template, letter, procedure…).

2
Fill in the document record Recommended

Set the type, domain, status and version. A unique reference is assigned automatically.

3
Search & filter

Use full-text search or filters (type, status, domain) to instantly find a document.

4
Consult & export

Open the document, review its metadata and export it to PDF / Excel if needed.

5
Archive & restore

Archive obsolete documents; they remain viewable and restorable to the main library.

Glossary

Key KILOJI terms

EDM
Electronic Document Management: a centralized library of the software’s documents.
CMMS
Computerized Maintenance Management System: planning and tracking of fleet maintenance.
HSEQ
Health, Safety, Environment and Quality: compliance and prevention mechanisms.
2FA
Two-factor authentication via an app (Google Authenticator) to secure login.
PPE
Personal Protective Equipment: safety gear tracked in stock and allocation.
Driving invariant
A monitored driving rule (speed, duration…) whose breach can generate a sanction.
Route
A planned vehicle trip, with loading point, distances, assignments and status.
Reference data
Configuration data (products, contract types, fleets, channels, manufacturers) feeding the modules.
Amendment
A contractual change attached to an existing driver contract.
Toolbox Meeting
A field safety talk: a short briefing documented in the HSEQ module.
